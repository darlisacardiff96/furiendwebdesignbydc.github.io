FuriendPet Health Tracker

Furiend is an app to track all your pet's information such as their food, supplement, medication, vaccination, and insurance. You will always have this information at your fingertips. Watch them as they grow.

Benefits from this app:

  • Manage your pet details
  • Manage unlimited number of food and supplement for each of your pet
  • Create reminders
  • Track medication and vaccine history
  • Capture important moments
  • Capture your pet's insurance
  • Sync with icloud
